Description


FIG. 1 is a front elevational view of a hooded facemask with a pivoting mouth piece, showing the design in a position of use on a human figure and with the mouth piece in a partially-raised position;

FIG. 2 is a front elevational view thereof, the mouth piece illustrated in a fully-raised position;

FIG. 3 is a front elevational view thereof, illustrated with a segment of the neck portion flipped upwards to expose an inside seam area of the neck portion;

FIG. 4 is a rear elevational view of FIG. 2;

FIG. 5 is a right-side elevational view of FIG. 1 shown when not in a position of use. The mouthpiece is shown in a partially raised position;

FIG. 6 is a left-side elevational view of FIG. 1 when not in a position of use. The mouth piece is shown in a partially raised position

FIG. 7 is a top plan view of FIG. 1;

FIG. 8 is a bottom plan view of FIG. 1; and,

FIG. 9 is a front elevational view thereof, illustrated with the mouth piece in a lowered position.

The broken lines showing a human figure and a logo in FIGS. 1, 5 and 9 depict environment that forms no part of the claimed design. All other broken lines depict stitching included in the claimed design.
